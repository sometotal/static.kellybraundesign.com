Home

Kelly Braun Design, LLC is an interior design consulting company based in Tacoma, Washington providing services to architects, design professionals and individuals.

For architects and design professionals, Kelly works as an integrated part of the design team with services such as programming, space planning, selecting finishes, specifying furniture, detailing millwork, creating 3D Sketch-up models and AutoCad drafting. Kelly also works with individuals to examine spatial issues, assist with color & furniture selection as well as graphic design services.  Please refer to the Experience for more details about the skills and experience she can bring to your project.

Her broad experience in commercial interiors equips her with knowledge of many project types from large public projects to small private spaces. The links under “Projects” at the side of the page will lead you to images and information about the projects in which she has been involved.

She approaches her work with dedication, professionalism, flexibility and good humor. Each client and project is unique and Kelly has the flexibility to work among various styles and capacities to address the needs of the project. If you need conceptual ideas for a small area, help with completing construction documents or someone to join a project team from schematic design to construction administration, Kelly can offer expertise.

Kelly is now employed by LMN Architects.
